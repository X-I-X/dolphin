Sissy saves drowning woman
A woman fakes
drowning. Sissy
immediately comes
to her rescue.
 Sissy puts her
right flipper under
her and pushes the
the woman to the
edge of the pool,
keeping her head
above water. 		Sissy doesn't let
let her back into the
pool again for
30 minutes.
