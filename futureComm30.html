Home›Writings›The Children

from the Future Communications Laboratory

A priority should be placed on couples who are willing to bear children. Pregnant women must be willing to swim with the dolphins, allowing the babies inside them to hear dolphin speech, be sonared, and acquire a nascent sense of the water. The dolphins will also benefit from close cohabitation with young families, seeing the basics of human existance. To them, we are the aliens, and their learning about how we live can only help to create context to build understanding and communication between the species. Hopefully, the dolphins will decide to bring their infants to the facility, so that all the children can play together. It will be these children that finally crack the code to human-dolphin communication.
