from the Future Communications Laboratory

6. The next level in the progression of dolphin to human habitation is an area of shallower pools honeycombed around platforms and a "wet house", an area of 18 inch deep water that contains human-style furniture. 7. The shallow pools finally lead indoors to the human house and room as near to a human-style "family room" will be created, allbeit with a pool. This room should have couches, a television and sound system, a dinner table, and close access to the kitchen. Another sleeping area, also alongside a pool, will also be in the co-habitational area of the human-house.
