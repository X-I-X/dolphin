from the Future Communications Laboratory

Seventy-one percent of the surface of the planet is the oceans. In these oceans live the cetaceans, dolphins, porpoises, and whales. The reasons to establish communication with them are

-Practice speaking with an alien intelligence

-Potential to learn from larger-brained beings

 

-Potential to learn about earth history (the dolphins have been here 25 million years longer than us)

-Potential to learn how to survive in peace and live in harmony with the environment.
