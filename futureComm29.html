from the Future Communications Laboratory

The human researchers required at this facility must be intelligent, open-minded, from various cultural backgrounds, and willing to integrate their lives into the interspecies experiment. They must be skilled in science and logic, yet not afraid to employ methods that are not yet proven by science, or that never will be. The team must be comprised of well-rounded individuals, but who each possess a specialty needed at the lab. Not only scientific minds, but artists, musicians, philosphers, spiritualists, and others who have come to see that the present human experience must change to a more global, peaceful and intelligent co-existance with our planet and the all residents there.
