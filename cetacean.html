Cetacean Nation

The study of dolphins, porpoises, and whales

ETs from Earth

It is common in this day and age to discuss the possibility of other intelligences in the universe. The discussion usually then goes to questions of UFOs and alien encounters. Most people are not aware that on our own planet exist intelligences that are at least as advanced as human — the Cetaceans. "More advanced, but in a very different way," says JCL. Based upon the criteria used by scientists to determine intelligence, many cetacean species are indeed more advanced than humans. When one considers that these beings have had brains as developed and even more so than humans for perhaps 50 to 100 million years longer, they merit the highest regard by us. The Cetacean Nation is the Earth's oceans, which comprise 70 percent of the surface of the planet. The senseless killing of cetaceans must cease. We must recognize and develop relations with the Cetacean Nation.

World's Largest Brain

The species on the Earth with the largest brain is the sperm whale. Its brain is six times the size of a human brain.
