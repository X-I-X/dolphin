5. The "Blue Zone". Pool C will have a recessed area with observation windows all around. Here technology will be focussed in the real task of establishing documentable communication with the dolphins.

 

 One window will actually be a large projection screen, capable of displaying a computer or video image, with touchscreen capabilities for the dolphins. This is used in coordination with the highest quality audio devices possible, including not only the best mics and speakers, but also spatial capabilities and realtime wave analysis.
