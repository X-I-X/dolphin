## **  
St. Thomas Laboratory 1960-1968**

By a series of coincidences John found the means to buy some land in a suitable location on the island of St. Thomas in the Caribbean Sea where he could build his new labaratory. The lab was constructed to create conditions suitable for close human/dolphin interaction.
