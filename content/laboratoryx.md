# St. Thomas Laboratory

St. Thomas, US  
Virgin Islands. The  
laboratory of the the  
Communications  
Research Institute  
Here the [conditons for  
human/dolphin co-  
existance](http://www.johnclilly.com/laboratory04x.html) were created.  
The pool is a  
sea-fed, sea water  
pool fed by the  
waves-- brought  
in through a  
wave-ramp, flooding  
the balcony of the  
lab to a depth of  
18 inches. Below the balcony  
is the main dolphin  
living space.  
The water changes  
every two hours,  
and as you can  
see, the dolphins  
are quite happy.
