John Cunningham Lilly

January 6, 1915 – September 30, 2001

John Cunningham Lilly passed to the other side on September 30, 2001. A giant of twentieth-century science and consciousness research, his legacy lives on through his writings, his discoveries, and the ongoing work of those who carry forward his vision of interspecies understanding.
