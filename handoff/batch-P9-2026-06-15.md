## Content notes

### About this chapter

*Center of the Cyclone* (1972) is Lilly's account of his inner explorations with LSD, isolation tanks, and the guides he encountered in altered states. Chapter 4, "Following Instructions and Going with the Flow," covers the 1964–1965 transition period: the tightening of LSD regulations, Lilly winding down his dolphin project in response to guide instructions, and his detailed experiments with the "repeating word" phenomenon — presenting a word on repeat to subjects to trigger auditory alternates (different heard words from the same phonetic sequence), and discovering how peripheral vision, suggestion, and intention could "program" which alternates the subject heard. This chapter bridges his experimental and mystical periods.
