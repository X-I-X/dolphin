### laboratory02.html — Slider unwrapped from anchor link

**Problem:** The entire slider widget was wrapped in an `<a href="laboratory.html">` anchor:
```html

  ...slider...
</div></a>
```
Clicking anywhere on the image would navigate away from the page rather than operating the slider. The back-navigation intent was valid but the implementation broke the slider UX.

**Fix:** Unwrapped the slider from the anchor. Added proper navigation as a separate `page-nav-return` paragraph:
```html

</div>

```
