## Content notes

### About LILLYLAND (lillyland00–12)
LILLYLAND was an interactive mini-site within johnclilly.com, built on Shockwave/Director. The landing page (lillyland00) introduced the concept with a Lilly quote and a single crater photo, then offered two paths: OUTER dimension (cogitatex.html) and INNER dimension (innerx.html). The interactive content itself is now static (innerx.html has been preserved as a Shockwave preservation note in a prior batch).

The long vertical scroll on lillyland00 (144+ `<br>` tags) is an intentional 1990s design gimmick: scroll far enough to find "What? You again?" with a link to lillyland10. lillyland10, 11, and 12 are purely image-grid pages — animated GIF tilings of johncrator02.gif, johncrator03.gif, and johnspiral02.gif respectively. These had no text content. The 1990s equivalent of a loading animation or visual "easter egg" sequence.

### About Lilly on Dolphins (lillyondolphins00–01)
"Lilly on Dolphins" is the archive page for Lilly's 1975 Anchor Books compilation: a single volume combining *Man and Dolphin*, *The Dolphin in History*, and *The Mind of the Dolphin*, plus two scientific papers. The site had two pages: an index (page 00 with `lillyondolphins01.gif` book cover) and a summary page (page 01 with `lillyondolphins02.gif` back cover + blockquote summary). Both have been restored with correct Cloudinary image paths and clean navigation.

### OCR errors in lillyondolphins01.html (preserved verbatim)
- "papaers" — original OCR error for "papers"
- "Soic" — original OCR error for "Sonic"
- Both preserved per content fidelity rules
