## Fixes applied

### 1. Archive-notice self-referential href fixed (all 26 pages)

All 26 pages had the archive-notice link `href` pointing back to the local filename rather than to johnclilly.com. Identical defect pattern to P11.

**Before:**
```html
<a href="womandolph01.html" rel="noopener" data-original-href="http://www.johnclilly.com/womandolph01.html">
```

**After:**
```html
<a href="http://www.johnclilly.com/womandolph01.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener" data-original-href="http://www.johnclilly.com/womandolph01.html">
```

Pattern applied to all 26 pages with their respective filenames.
