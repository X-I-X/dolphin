## Fixes applied

### 1. Archive-notice self-referential href fixed (all 11 pages)

All 11 pages had the archive-notice link `href` pointing back to themselves rather than to johnclilly.com. The `data-original-href` and display text were already correct; only the `href` attribute was wrong.

**Before:**
```html
<a href="dolphart01.html" rel="noopener" data-original-href="http://www.johnclilly.com/dolphart01.html">
```

**After:**
```html
<a href="http://www.johnclilly.com/dolphart01.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener" data-original-href="http://www.johnclilly.com/dolphart01.html">
```

Pattern applied to all 11 pages with their respective filenames.
