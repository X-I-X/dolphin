from the Future Communications Laboratory

4. Pool C This pool ranges from a deep end of approximately 4 meters in depth, to a large area of 1 meter depth, with access canals leading from this area in to the more human levels of the lab. This pool will contain many observation windows, and the so called "blue zone" where the focus of the lab's technology will be located. It is assumed that the majority of "water sports" will be done in this area. This pool will be large enough for the dolphins to swim comfortably and engage with the humans. The "far side" of this pool would link to the an area accessible by the general public.
