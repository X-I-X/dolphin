John C. Lilly

Scientist · Explorer · Visionary
January 6, 1915 – September 30, 2001

John Cunningham Lilly, M.D. — an unparalleled scientific visionary whose work launched the global interest in dolphins and whales, invented the isolation tank, and pioneered the theory that the human brain operates as a biological computer. His dozen books have sold millions of copies worldwide.
